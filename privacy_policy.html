Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2025  |  Last updated: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Relatos del Abismo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our application and services ("the Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you connect your TikTok account to our Service.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the Service. The following table summarizes what we collect and why:

Data Type Source Purpose Storage
OAuth Access Token TikTok Authorization Authenticate API calls to upload video content Local device only
TikTok User ID TikTok API Identify the account for publishing Local device only
TikTok Username TikTok API Confirm correct account is connected Local device only

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Financial or payment information
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Contact lists, messages, or social graph data
  • Viewing history or behavioral analytics
  • Any data from users other than the authenticated account holder

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain an authorized session with the TikTok API on your behalf.
  • Content Publishing: To upload and publish video content to your TikTok account as explicitly directed by you.
  • Service Operation: To ensure the proper functioning of the connection between our Service and your TikTok account.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, user profiling, analytics, machine learning training, or any purpose other than those stated above.

4. Data Storage and Security

All authentication tokens and account identifiers are stored exclusively on your local device. We do not operate any remote servers, databases, or cloud storage that retains your personal information.

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, including:

  • Local storage of tokens with access limited to the application process
  • Use of HTTPS for all API communications with TikTok
  • No logging or transmission of authentication credentials to external servers

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes.

The only external service your data interacts with is TikTok, through its official API. When you authorize the Service, TikTok processes your data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy, which can be found at: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or in response to a valid legal request from governmental authorities.

6. TikTok API Data Use Compliance

Our use of data obtained through the TikTok API strictly complies with TikTok's API Terms of Service and Developer Policies. Specifically:

  • We only request the minimum API scopes necessary to provide the Service (video upload and basic account info)
  • We do not use TikTok API data for purposes beyond the functionality described in this policy
  • We do not transfer TikTok user data to third parties
  • We do not use TikTok data to build profiles of users or track user behavior across services

7. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained on your local device for as long as you continue to use the Service or until you revoke access. You can remove all stored data at any time by:

  • Revoking the application's access through TikTok's account settings under "Manage app permissions"
  • Deleting the locally stored token file from your device
  • Uninstalling or removing the application

Upon revocation or deletion, all associated tokens become invalid and no further API calls can be made on your behalf.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your personal data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 11.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.
